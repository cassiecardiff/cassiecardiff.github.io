About Me

Hi, I'm Cassandra (Cassie for short). I'm an experienced and socially conscious yoga teacher with a passion for making dynamic yoga more accessible.

Trained to teach Baptiste-Inspired Power Flow, Core Strength Vinyasa and Yoga For All, my classes are playful and powerful with intelligently cued postures that will bring you into efficient, safe and stable core-generated alignment tailored to you. My focus is on playfully adjusting postures and flows to meet the needs and body of the yoga practitioner - not the other way around.

I'm a Yoga For All certified teacher and work hard to ensure that my classes are accessible to everybody, regardless of body shape, size, injury or ability. I'm happy to work with you to ensure you get the most out of each and every yoga practice.
